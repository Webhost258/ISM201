Financing your journey

Financing flight school can be hard and most pilots need a way to get flight hours in quick without having to work on the side to earn money. One way is through Meritize Loans which you can check out here.

Liberty University

Liberty University offers a bachelor's degree in Aviation and partners with Cornerstone Aviation to provide flight school. Tution is similar to BYU Provo and Cornerstone is an estabilshed program.

Air Force

The Air Force. will pay all of your flight school expenses in return for years of service. If you are interested in flying high performance aircraft than this is the choice for you.

Utah State University

USU is another aviation institution that focuses on agriculture and commerce.
